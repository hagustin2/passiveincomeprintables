What are Printables?

Printables are digital products that people can buy and print at home or at a print shop. They include things like wall art, planners, and invitations. When you sell printables as passive earnings, you’re offering something that your customers can easily access and use without waiting for a physical item to be shipped. This makes printables a perfect product for online businesses, especially if you want to generate passive earnings.

Why Sell Printables?

Printables or digital downloads are pieces of art or designs that customers can buy and print themselves at home. They are easy to use and come in various formats like wall art, invitations, planners, and more. These products are great because they don’t require shipping, and customers can use them immediately after purchase. Printables as passive earnings are becoming increasingly popular due to low upfront costs and the potential to earn recurring revenue from a single design.
