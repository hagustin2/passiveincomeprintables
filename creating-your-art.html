Seasonal Prints 101

Your Guide to Selling Seasonal Printables on Etsy

Start Creating Your Art

Creating digital art can be fun and easy, especially when you’re designing printables to sell on Etsy.

Pick a Style for Your Art

The first thing to do is decide what kind of art you want to make. Do you want to create designs with quotes, fun illustrations, or home decorations? Picking a style helps you stay focused and makes it easier to attract the right people who will want to buy your art.

Use Easy Design Tools

You don’t need to be an expert to create good digital art. There are many simple tools you can use that make it easy to create beautiful designs, even if you're new to it.

Include Seasonal Designs

People love buying seasonal art for their homes or events! Adding seasonal designs is a great way to get more sales.
