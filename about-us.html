About Us

Hi, I'm Christine Yu, the founder of Passive Income Printables. After trying out different ways to make money online, I discovered that selling printables on Etsy was the best fit for me. What started as a small project quickly turned into something I loved, and I realized that passive income printables could be an excellent way to earn money with minimal effort.

I studied graphic design in school, which sparked my interest in creating digital products. Over the years, I focused on creating designs that bring in steady income without requiring a lot of active work. After learning the ins and outs of this business, I'm excited to share my knowledge with you. I’ve built a successful Etsy shop, selling thousands of printables that help people celebrate special moments in their lives.

This website provides you with simple, effective guides and tips for starting and growing your own Etsy shop. Whether you’re new to the world of passive income printables or want to scale your existing shop, I’m here to help you build a business that grows over time.
