Printables as Passive Income

Your Guide to Earning with Digital Products

Why Etsy?

Etsy is a popular marketplace for handmade, vintage, and digital products. It’s a great place to sell your printables because of its large and engaged audience. People visit Etsy looking for unique and creative items, making it an ideal platform for selling your digital art and printables. Setting up an Etsy shop is easy and affordable, with built-in tools to help you manage your products, customers, and orders.

Setting Up Your Etsy Account

  • Sign up: Visit Etsy.com and click "Sign up" to create a new account.
  • Create a Seller Account: After signing in, click on "Sell on Etsy" to start setting up your seller account.
  • Choose a Shop Name: Pick a unique name for your Etsy shop that represents your brand or product style. Make sure it’s easy to remember!
  • Set Up Payment and Billing Information: You’ll need to add your payment details to get paid and set up a billing method for any Etsy fees.

Creating Your Shop Listings

  • Title: Choose a clear and descriptive title for your printable or digital art.
  • Photos: Upload high-quality images of your products. Make sure your photos show the details of your design clearly.
  • Description: Write a simple, straightforward description of what customers will get when they purchase your item. Mention what type of file formats they will receive (e.g., PDF, PNG, JPG).
  • Price: Set a price for your product. Consider the time and effort you put into creating your design.
  • Tags & Categories: Use relevant keywords (tags) to help customers find your products. Choose the correct category for your printable or digital art, such as "Art & Collectibles" or "Home Décor."
